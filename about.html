About LDA

Life-Long Development Academy (LDA) is an entrepreneurial training school focused on equipping students with practical and professional skills for career and self-employment opportunities.

Our programs combine theory with hands-on learning in multiple vocational and technical areas.

At Life-Long Development Academy (LDA), we believe that education should not only inform the mind but also empower individuals with practical skills that create opportunities for life. Our academy is built on the principle that every individual has the potential to become productive, innovative and self-reliant when given the right guidance and environment.
